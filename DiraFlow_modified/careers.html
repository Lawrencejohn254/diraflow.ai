How we hire

Our interview process

01

Application review ~1 week

We read every application carefully. If it's a fit, we'll reach out within a week for an initial conversation.

02

Intro call 30 min

A relaxed conversation with a team member to learn about you, your background, and answer any questions you have about Diraflow.

03

Skills assessment 2–4 hrs, paid

A focused, paid take-home task that reflects real work you'd do in the role. We respect your time and compensate for it fairly.

04

Team interviews 2 conversations

Two 45-minute conversations: one technical deep-dive, one culture and values conversation with future teammates.

05

Offer within 48 hrs

We move quickly. Offer includes full compensation details and dedicated time to ask any remaining questions before you decide.
